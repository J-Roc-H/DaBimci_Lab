# Hello, I'm Da Bimci 👋

**"Where the master's curiosity meets the machine's efficiency."**

I am an architectural designer and BIM specialist with 9 years of experience. I bridge the gap between creative architectural vision and digital precision by developing custom automation tools. My focus is on transforming repetitive manual workflows into streamlined, data-driven processes.

## 🛠 Areas of Expertise
*   **BIM & Design:** Advanced Revit workflows, Navisworks integration, and model management.
*   **Automation:** Developing custom scripts for architectural tasks using Python (pyRevit) and Dynamo (Visual Programming).
*   **Workflow Optimization:** Enhancing productivity through custom template development and automated data extraction.

## 🚀 My Philosophy
I believe that by automating the routine aspects of documentation and coordination, architects are empowered to dedicate more time to the art of design. This repository serves as my archive for tools, scripts, and experiments developed to refine the practice of architecture.

## 💻 Tech Stack
